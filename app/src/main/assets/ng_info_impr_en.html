Data privacy
Capricorn will not collect personal data. Data are collected only to display in the app or the widgets and furthermore not stored permanently and not forwarded to any third party.

Capricorn has no access to your contacts.

If you want you can enter here phone numbers which are free of charge for you.
Capricorn will not use calls to this numbers to calculate your used free minutes.
Capricorn does not forward these numbers to any third party, does not know the owner of these numbers and will use these numbers only for the internal calculations.

Therefore these numbers are not person related data for Capricorn After de-installation of Capricorn all data inclusive these phone numbers will be deleted entirely.
